# Privacy Policy Draft

Reviewed: 2026-06-08

This draft is for Mtendere Education Consult's website and digital platform. It should be reviewed by qualified counsel before publication as a final legal notice.

## 1. Who We Are

Mtendere Education Consult provides education guidance, scholarship and opportunity discovery, application support, events, career services, referrals, communications, and related digital services.

Contact:

- Email: mtendereeducation@gmail.com
- Phone: +265 999 360 325
- Location: Lilongwe, Malawi

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Account details: name, email, phone, username, password hash, role, profile details, and verification status.
- Application details: education history, work history, references, personal statements, uploaded documents, opportunity choices, and application status.
- Communications: contact form messages, support tickets, email preferences, subscription records, delivery events, and consent records.
- Usage and device information: IP address, browser details, pages visited, timestamps, security events, and analytics data.
- Payment and referral data where applicable: checkout identifiers, referral activity, payout requests, and related transaction metadata.
- Security information: login attempts, MFA status, token events, audit logs, and abuse-prevention signals.

## 3. Why We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Create, verify, and protect accounts.
- Provide requested education, application, event, referral, and support services.
- Process applications and uploaded documents.
- Send service, verification, password reset, application, event, and subscription messages.
- Improve service quality, platform reliability, and user experience.
- Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and security incidents.
- Keep records required for legal, audit, dispute, operational, or security purposes.

## 4. Legal and Consent Basis

Depending on the context, we process information because:

- The processing is needed to provide a requested service.
- The user has given consent.
- The processing supports legitimate operational, safety, or service-improvement interests.
- The processing is required for legal, regulatory, audit, or dispute purposes.

Where consent is used, users may request withdrawal of consent. Withdrawal may affect services that rely on that consent.

## 5. Sharing Information

We may share information with:

- Authorized Mtendere staff and administrators.
- Education institutions, partners, or service providers involved in a requested application or opportunity.
- Email, hosting, analytics, security, database, payment, and communication service providers.
- Professional advisers where needed.
- Public authorities, regulators, courts, or law enforcement where legally required.

We do not sell personal information.

## 6. International Transfers

Some service providers, institutions, or partners may be outside Malawi. Where information is transferred internationally, Mtendere should use appropriate safeguards, contractual controls, and service-provider due diligence.

## 7. Cookies and Analytics

The platform may use essential local storage, cookies, or similar technologies for authentication, security, preferences, analytics, and service improvement. Analytics should be configured to collect only what is necessary for platform performance and business insight.

## 8. Security

Mtendere uses safeguards such as password hashing, access controls, HTTPS-oriented deployment, security headers, rate limiting, audit logs, MFA support for sensitive roles, upload restrictions, and operational monitoring. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but security incidents should be assessed, contained, documented, and escalated appropriately.

## 9. Retention

Mtendere keeps information only as long as needed for the service purpose, account relationship, application workflow, legal compliance, audit, dispute resolution, security, or operational continuity. A formal retention schedule should define retention periods by record type.

## 10. User Rights

Subject to identity verification and applicable law, users may request:

- Access to personal information.
- Correction of inaccurate information.
- Deletion where legally and operationally possible.
- Restriction or objection to certain processing.
- Portability where applicable.
- Withdrawal of consent.
- Review of a privacy complaint.

Requests can be sent to mtendereeducation@gmail.com.

## 11. Children and Students

Mtendere services may involve students. Where a user is under the age required by applicable law to consent independently, a parent, guardian, or authorized representative may be required.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

Mtendere may update this policy as services, laws, or operational practices change. Material updates should be published with a new review date.
