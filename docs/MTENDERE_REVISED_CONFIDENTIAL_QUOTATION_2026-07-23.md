## 1. Current System Evaluation

A review of the current Mtendere Education Consult platform confirms that the delivered system has moved beyond a basic website. It is now a functional digital education management and operations platform prepared for handover to the Mtendere team for production use and day-to-day operations.

The system currently includes public-facing services, student/client workflows, administrative management tools, backend integration, authentication, content management, application processing, communication workflows, documentation, and production deployment configuration.

> The current system should be valued and presented as a complete digital operations platform, not as a simple website project.

The current platform is prepared for handover to the Mtendere team for production and operational use.
